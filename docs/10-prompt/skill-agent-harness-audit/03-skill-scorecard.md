## Scorecard Notes

- `spec-skill-audit`, `spec-graph-bootstrap`, `spec-write-tasks`, and `spec-doc-review` are the best current templates for Harness-style structure.
- Low scores for `using-spec-first`, `spec-work-beta`, `spec-optimize`, `spec-ideate`, and `spec-brainstorm` are partly structural false negatives because these files contain rich workflow prose but do not expose a compact normalized contract at the top.
- The rewrite target is not longer Markdown. The minimum fix is a stable contract header that makes trigger, inputs, outputs, artifacts, degraded mode, safety, and handoff scannable before deep workflow details.
- Semantic P0s are not driven by low score alone. They come from public/internal boundary conflict, legacy central autonomous orchestration, or hidden mutating helper exposure.
